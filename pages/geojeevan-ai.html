GeoJeevan: When Data Becomes an Early Warning.

We built something real. Your environment, analysed in real time. This is not just data — this is an early warning system.

GeoJeevan AI 🌍

It analyzes your environment and tells you your actual health risk in real time.

Example from today:
→ Overall Risk: 43%
→ Air Quality Impact: 60%
→ Heat + Dengue risk included

This is not just data. This is early warning.

Why this matters

India has a wealth of environmental data — AQI dashboards, dengue bulletins, heat index maps. But none of it reaches the person who needs it, in a format they can actually use, at the moment that matters.

A raw AQI number means nothing to most people. "AQI: 187" doesn't tell you whether to send your child to school today. It doesn't tell you whether your morning run is putting you at risk. It doesn't connect air quality to your heat exposure or your local disease burden.

GeoJeevan connects all of it. The AI synthesises environmental signals into one number with one clear meaning: here is your health risk, right now, in your location.

What we built

The platform pulls from air quality, temperature and heat index, regional disease prevalence data, and seasonal risk patterns. The AI model produces a single risk score broken down by contributing factor. You see not just how much risk, but what's causing it.

This is an early-stage product. We're being honest about that. The model is working. The scores are real. What we need now is scale — more users, more feedback, more edge cases to sharpen the AI against.

The honest question

We launched by asking a direct question to the LinkedIn community: Would you trust this enough to use it daily? Or what's missing for you?

That question isn't rhetorical. The answer determines what we build next. If you have used GeoJeevan and have feedback — about what's wrong, what's missing, what would make you open it every morning the way you open a weather app — I want to hear it.
