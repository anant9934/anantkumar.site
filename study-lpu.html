Case Study

From personal frustration
to a platform 3,000+ trust.

01 · Problem

Students were drowning in unstructured preparation.

Before every LPU midterm, the ritual was the same: frantically search WhatsApp groups for PDFs, download 40 MB files that may or may not be for the right subject, find old question papers from three semesters ago, and hope for the best.

No structured MCQ environment. No exam timer. No way to track improvement. Students weren't failing because they were lazy — the tools were broken.

02 · Solution

Simulate the real exam. Beat it before you sit it.

Study LPU gives every student unit-wise quizzes, a custom exam generator, a real timer, live leaderboards, verified resources, and AI-powered mocks — all in one place.

We added a mentorship layer on top: 9+ expert mentors, flexible pay-per-session. Because sometimes the best resource is a person.

AI Mock Tests MCQ Engine Leaderboards Mentorship Resource Hub Web Platform
04 · Outcome

3,000+ students. Built entirely on word of mouth.

Not through paid acquisition. Not through ads. Through students telling other students — because they actually felt the difference in their results.

Subjects currently supported: MTH166, ECE249, CSE111, CHE110, PHY110. Architecture designed to onboard new subjects in hours, not weeks. Next milestone: 5,000 active students.
