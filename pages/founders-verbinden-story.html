🚀 Founders, Builders, Dreamers —

What's the one thing slowing your startup right now? Funding? Tech? Team? Clarity?

💡 Real connections. 💡 Real execution. 💡 Real growth.

That's the promise at Founders Verbinden. Not connections that look good on LinkedIn. Connections that hold weight when things get hard.

Why I built this

I've been in rooms — both virtual and physical — that claim to be for founders. Most of them are networking theatre. Business cards exchanged. Pitches rehearsed. Nothing built.

The founders I've spoken to don't need more introductions. They need someone who has solved the exact problem they're stuck on — and will actually spend two hours working through it with them, not just give them a podcast recommendation.

That's the gap Founders Verbinden is built to close. A community where the floor entry is seriousness. Where the output is measurable. Where the promise is: if you show up and engage, something real will happen.

What we're building

The vision: acceleration through expert mentors, structured collaboration on real problems, a curated network of vetted co-founders and investors, and a resource library built by practitioners who have actually shipped things.

We're also building the underlying platform — currently hiring frontend, backend, and full-stack developers who want to build something that matters, on a real team, from the ground up.

The question I'm sitting with

What is the one thing slowing your startup right now? I ask this not as a rhetorical prompt but because the answer shapes everything we build next.

We're listening. And building for whoever drops their honest answer.
