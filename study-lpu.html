01 · Problem

Students were drowning in unstructured preparation.

Before every LPU midterm, the ritual was the same: frantically search WhatsApp groups for PDFs, download 40 MB files from Google Drive that may or may not be for the right subject, find old question papers from three semesters ago, and hope for the best.

There was no structured MCQ environment. No exam timer that simulated real pressure. No way to track whether you were actually improving. No reliable way to know what questions mattered most. Students weren't failing because they were lazy — they were failing because the tools they had were broken.

I noticed this during my own midterm season. I had the same frustrations as everyone else. The difference was I had the technical ability to do something about it.

02 · Solution

Simulate the real exam. Beat it before you sit it.

Study LPU Online is built around one core insight: the best way to prepare for an exam is to practice under conditions that feel like the real exam. Not reading notes. Not watching videos. Doing.

The platform gives every LPU student access to unit-wise quizzes (Easy / Medium / Hard), a custom exam generator where you choose the subject, difficulty, number of questions, and duration, a real exam timer environment with MCQ interface, live leaderboards to compete with peers, verified study resources uploaded and reviewed by toppers and mentors, and AI-powered mock tests that adapt to your weak areas.

We added a mentorship layer — 9+ expert mentors available for 1:1 sessions with a flexible pay-per-session model. Because sometimes what a student needs isn't another resource. It's another human who has already solved the problem they're stuck on.

03 · Technology

Built fast. Iterated faster.

The platform was built with a strong emphasis on speed and simplicity — because the primary users are students under exam pressure. Every millisecond of friction is a reason to close the tab.

Subjects currently supported: MTH166, ECE249, CSE111, CHE110, PHY110. The architecture is designed to onboard new subjects in hours, not weeks.

04 · Outcome

300 students in. 1,000 is the next milestone.

Study LPU crossed 300 active students faster than expected. Not through paid acquisition — through word of mouth, because students actually felt the difference in their results. The post announcing this milestone received real engagement from a community that rarely celebrates EdTech products.

The next target is 1,000+ active students. The platform continues to grow with new subjects, new mentors, and new features. The mission hasn't changed: make exam preparation structured, competitive, and efficient for every LPU student who deserves better tools.
